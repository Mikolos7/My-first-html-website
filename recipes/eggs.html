Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Descriptiom

A very simple and flexible recipe of eggs fried and scrambled to one's heart content

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter or groundnutoil
  • An onion bulb
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Break the eggs into a bowl
  2. Chop up the onion and pepper
  3. Add into the bowl of eggs
  4. Add the milk(and any other thing you deem necessary)
  5. Whisk the mix
  6. Put some oil(2 table spoons) in the frying pan and heat up
  7. Pour your mix into it
  8. Scramble with a spoon as it fries up
  9. Dish out and gorge yourself because you deserve it
