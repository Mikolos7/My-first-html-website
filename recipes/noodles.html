Noodles Recipe

Description

An exquisite noodle recipe made from scratch with tomatoes, pepper and onions. Add some seasoning to accentuate the flavour.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • A serving spoon of groundnut oil
  • A small onion bulb
  • A table spoon of tomato sauce
  • Pepper(either fresh or processed)
  • A tin of sardine or geisha
  • Carrots
  • An egg

Steps

  1. Boil the egg for 15mins
  2. Place your pot on the cooker at full heat
  3. Put the grpundnut oil in the pot for 45secs
  4. Chop up the onion and put it in
  5. Immediately follow up with the tomato sauce
  6. Let it fry till it seems to be bubbling up when you pick it up with your spoon
  7. Put in the noodles
  8. Add an appropriate amount of water relative to the amount of noodles being prepared
  9. Chop up the carrot, put in the pot and let it boil for a minute
  10. Put in the seasoing powder if your noodle pack comes with one but if it doesn't, use seasoning cubes. A seasoning cube per noodle pack should suffice
  11. Stir the whole mixture and let it simmer for a while
  12. Add the tin of sardine or geisha(I prefer geisha) and stir
  13. Let it simmer till you attain your desired amount of moisture in the noodles
  14. Dish it out with the egg and gorge yourself because you deserve it
